# RWDR External Feedback Checklist

Use this checklist in guided sessions with selected manufacturers, distributors, application engineers, or technical sales experts.

## Reviewer Questions

1. Would this `Technical RWDR RFQ Brief` help you respond faster than a normal customer email?
2. Which required RWDR fields are still missing?
3. Which questions are unnecessary?
4. Which questions are most valuable?
5. Would inside sales or application engineering use this?
6. Would you trust the source-span confirmation model?
7. Is the disclaimer clear without being too defensive?
8. Is the PDF professional enough to receive from a customer?
9. Does anything look like a material, product, or manufacturer recommendation?
10. What would make this brief quote-ready?
11. What would make you reject this inquiry?
12. Which fields should be mandatory for your company?
13. Would this reduce clarification loops?
14. Would you want this as a supplier intake standard?
15. What is the strongest missing capability?
